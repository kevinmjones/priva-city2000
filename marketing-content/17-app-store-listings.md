## 1. Measuring AR

### App Store Title (iOS, 30 char)
Measuring AR: Tape Measure

### Short Description (Android, 80 char)
AR measurement tool. Real-time dimensions. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

### Description

Point your camera, measure anything. Measuring AR gives you real-time dimensions using augmented reality — no tape measure, no account setup, no data collection.

**What it does:**
- Measure distance, area, and height in real time
- See dimension lines overlaid on your camera view
- Save and share measurements

**Why you'll keep it:**
Every other AR measuring tool wants your data, your location, or your email. Measuring AR needs none of it. The measurement happens on your device, stays on your device, and disappears when you close the app. No signup. No cloud. No tracking. No ads.

**Privacy:** This app collects zero data. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry. Code is open source — verify it yourself.

### Keywords (iOS, 100 char)
measuring,tape measure,AR,ruler,measurement,dimensions,room measure,floor plan

## 4. Translation

### App Store Title (iOS, 30 char)
Translation: On-Device

### Short Description (Android, 80 char)
Translate text between languages. On-device. Private. No tracking.

### Description

Type or paste text and get instant translations. Everything happens on your device — your words never leave it. No cloud processing. No training data collection. No privacy policy that says "we may share your data with third parties."

**What it does:**
- On-device translation (nothing sent to a server)
- Multiple language pairs
- Clean, distraction-free interface
- Works offline (download language packs once)

**Why you'll keep it:**
Cloud-based translation services read everything you translate. For personal messages, private documents, or sensitive content, that's a problem. Translation keeps your words on your phone, where they belong.

Star Gaze: Night Sky Guide

Point at the night sky. Identify stars, constellations, planets. Zero ads.

### Description

Hold your phone to the sky and Star Gaze identifies stars, constellations, planets, and satellites in real time. Constellation guides help you navigate the night sky. Object tracking follows celestial bodies as they move.

**What it does:**
