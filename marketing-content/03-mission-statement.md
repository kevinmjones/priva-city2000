# Mission Statement: Arch Apps

> **We build software that respects your attention, your data, and your intelligence.**

Arch Apps exists because the software industry drifted. Somewhere along the way, tools became platforms, products became data-collection engines, and every app demanded a relationship.

We think a tool should do its job — then get out of your way.

### Core Principles

- **Simplicity first** — every feature must prove it makes the tool better at its primary job. If it doesn't, it doesn't ship.
- **Offline by default** — if an app doesn't need the internet, it shouldn't require it. Your data stays on your device.
- **Privacy as architecture** — we design for zero data collection from day one, not as an afterthought or a premium feature.
- **One price, fair terms** — free or buy once, own forever. No subscriptions, no upsells, no "premium" that is actually the real product.
- **Built in the open** — our code is public, our decisions are transparent, and anyone can verify what we claim.

### The Long View

We are not looking for a quick exit. Arch Apps is built to last — as a sustainable collection of tools people can rely on for years. We grow by being useful, not by being extractive.

Small is sustainable. Small is honest. Small means every user matters.
