# Announcing Arch Apps: Software That Stays Out of Your Way

**Date:** June 2026
**Author:** Arch Apps Team

---

We're launching Arch Apps — a collection of simple, functional tools that do one thing well. No subscriptions. No data mining. No feature bloat. Just honest software that works, then gets out of your way.

## Why We Built This

The software industry has a problem. Somewhere along the way, we decided that every tool should be a platform, every app should demand a relationship, and every "free" product should come with a hidden cost.

A unit converter doesn't need your email address. A spirit level doesn't need your location. A translation app shouldn't sell your text to a training dataset. And none of them should charge you every month for the privilege of using a tool you could replace with a piece of wood and some markings.

But that's the world we live in. Apps that started as simple utilities now ship with chat, AI assistants, social feeds, and advertising networks. The business model is the product, and the user is the inventory.

We think that's wrong.

## What We're Building

Arch Apps is five tools, each designed for one job:

### Measuring AR
AR-powered measurement tools. Point your camera at something, see how big it is. No accounts, no cloud sync, no signup — just your camera and a tape measure that lives in your pocket.

### Unit Conversion
Convert anything to anything. Speed, temperature, volume, currency — instant results, zero friction. Works offline. Always free.

### Level
A precision spirit level using your device sensors. Calibrated, clean, and surprisingly accurate for something that costs nothing.

### Translation
Translate text between languages with a clean interface and no tracking. Your words stay on your device because the translation happens there.

### Star Gaze
Point your phone at the night sky and see what's up there. Constellation guides, object tracking, and zero ads blocking your view.

## What Makes Us Different

**Offline by default.** None of our apps require an internet connection to function. Your data stays on your device because there's nowhere for it to go.

**Privacy as architecture.** We built for zero data collection from day one. It's not a feature we added after launch — it's the foundation every app is built on.

**One price, fair terms.** Free for basic use. Pay once for premium features. Own it forever. No subscriptions, no upsells, no "premium" plans that are actually the real product.

**Built in the open.** Our code is public. Anyone can read it, audit it, or fork it. We have nothing to hide — and we mean that literally.

## Our Philosophy

Every app we build answers one question: *what is the simplest thing that works?*

We don't ask for your email. We don't sell your data. We don't hide features behind a paywall. Each app is a single-purpose tool — the digital equivalent of a well-made hammer or a sharp knife.

If that sounds refreshingly boring, good. That's the point.

Software should be a tool, not a relationship.
