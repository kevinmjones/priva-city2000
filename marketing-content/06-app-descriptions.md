## Measuring AR

**Short:** Point, measure, done. AR-powered measurement with no accounts, no cloud sync, no fuss.

**Long:** Open the app, point your camera at anything, and see its dimensions in real time. No setup, no calibration dance, no account creation. Just your camera and a tape measure that lives in your pocket.

**Why choose this over alternatives:** Every other AR measuring tool wants your data, your location, or your email. Measuring AR needs none of it — the measurement happens on your device, stays on your device, and disappears when you close the app. No signup. No cloud. No tracking.

**Key features:**
- Real-time AR measurement
- No account required
- Works fully offline
- Zero data collection
- Save and share measurements

## Unit Conversion

**Short:** Convert anything to anything. Speed, temperature, volume, currency — instant, offline, always free.

**Long:** Need to convert 37°C to Fahrenheit? How about 50 kilometers per hour to miles per hour? Or 250 milliliters to cups? Unit Conversion handles every category you can think of — instantly, without an internet connection, and without an ad popping up mid-calculation.

**Why choose this over alternatives:** Most converters are either ad-riddled or require internet access. Unit Conversion works offline with every unit we support, and we support a lot of them. No ads, no requests for data, no premium-gated features.

**Key features:**
- Comprehensive unit categories
- Works offline
- No ads, no tracking
- Instant results
- Frequently used conversions pinned for quick access

## Level

**Short:** A precision spirit level using your device sensors. Calibrated, clean, and surprisingly accurate.

**Long:** Turn your phone into a precision spirit level. The Level app uses your device's built-in sensors to measure angles and surfaces with surprising accuracy. Calibrate it once and it's ready to hang shelves, level furniture, or check that perfect picture frame.

**Why choose this over alternatives:** Built-in level tools on most phones are basic afterthoughts buried in other apps. Dedicated apps are full of ads. Level is a focused tool — calibration presets, angle readouts, and a clean interface. That's it.

**Key features:**
- Precision angle measurement
- Calibration presets
- Clean, focused interface
- No ads, no permissions beyond sensors
- Works offline

## Translation

**Short:** Translate text between languages. On-device. Private. No tracking.

**Long:** Type or paste text and get instant translations in multiple languages. Everything happens on your device — your words never leave it. No cloud processing, no training data collection, no privacy policy that says "we may share your data with third parties."

**Why choose this over alternatives:** Cloud-based translation services read everything you translate. For personal messages, private documents, or sensitive content, that's a problem. Translation keeps your words on your phone, where they belong.

**Key features:**
- On-device translation (no cloud)
- Multiple language pairs
- No data collection
- Clean, distraction-free interface
- Works offline

## Star Gaze

**Short:** Point your phone at the night sky and see what's up there. No ads blocking your view — literally.

**Long:** Hold your phone to the sky and Star Gaze identifies stars, constellations, planets, and satellites in real time. Constellation guides help you navigate the night sky, and object tracking follows celestial bodies as they move. The view is clean, informative, and — unlike every other stargazing app — completely ad-free.

**Why choose this over alternatives:** Popular stargazing apps are notorious for ads that block the very night sky they're supposed to help you see. Star Gaze shows you the stars, period. No "premium" subscription to remove ads. No data collection. Just the sky.

**Key features:**
- Real-time sky identification
- Constellation guides
- Object tracking
- Completely ad-free
- No account required
- Works offline (download sky data beforehand)
