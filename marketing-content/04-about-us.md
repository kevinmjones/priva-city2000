# About Arch Apps

## Our Story

Arch Apps started with a frustration that wouldn't go away: why does a simple tool need an account? Why does a unit converter need internet access? Why does a measuring app need my location?

The answer, we realized, is that most software is built for the people paying for it — not the people using it. Investors want recurring revenue. Platforms want engagement data. Marketers want funnel conversions.

We wanted something different. So we built it.

## The Flat-Cost Philosophy

**No bloat.** Every feature must earn its place. If it doesn't make the tool better at its primary job, it doesn't ship. This applies to UI, code, and business model alike.

**No subscriptions.** You shouldn't rent a hammer. You buy it once and it works. Our apps follow the same principle — pay once (or use them free), own them forever.

**No data mining.** We don't track you. We don't profile you. We don't sell access to you. Your data stays on your device because our apps don't need a server to function.

## What We Build

Five tools, one philosophy:

| App | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Measuring AR** | AR-powered measurement. Point, measure, done. No accounts, no cloud sync. |
| **Unit Conversion** | Convert anything to anything. Offline. Instant. Always free. |
| **Level** | A precision spirit level using your device sensors. Calibrated and clean. |
| **Translation** | Translate text between languages. On-device. No tracking. |
| **Star Gaze** | Point your phone at the night sky and see what's up there. No ads blocking your view. |

## The Team

A small group of engineers and designers who believe software can be better. We're not a startup chasing growth — we're a workshop building tools we actually want to use.

We work in the open. Our code is public. Our process is transparent. Our only metric is whether the tool helps you do what you need to do.
