About Arch Apps

How we started, what we believe, and why every line of code has a purpose.

Our story

Arch Apps started with a frustration that would not go away: why does a simple tool need an account? Why does a unit converter need internet access? Why does a measurement app need my location?

The answer, we realised, is that most software is built for the people paying for it — not the people using it. Investors want recurring revenue. Platforms want engagement data. Marketers want funnel conversions.

We wanted something different. So we built it.

The flat-cost philosophy

No bloat

Every feature must earn its place. If it does not make the tool better at its primary job, it does not ship. This applies to UI, code, and business model alike.

No subscriptions

You should not rent a hammer. You buy it once and it works. Our apps follow the same principle — pay once (or use them free), own them forever.

No data mining

We do not track you. We do not profile you. We do not sell access to you. Your data stays on your device because our apps do not need a server to function.

The team

A small group of engineers and designers who believe software can be better. We are not a startup chasing growth — we are a workshop building tools we actually want to use.

We work in the open. Our code is on GitHub. Our process is transparent. Our only metric is whether the tool helps you do what you need to do.
