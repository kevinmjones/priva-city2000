Why Arch Apps exists

Software should be a tool, not a relationship.

Our purpose

We build software for people who just want to get something done. The world is full of apps that demand your attention, your data, your time, and your money — then still get in your way.

Arch Apps exists because the industry drifted. Somewhere along the way, the software industry decided that the user is the product, that endless engagement is the goal, and that every tool should be a platform.

We think that is wrong. A tool should do its job and then disappear. A measuring app should measure. A converter should convert. A translation tool should translate. Nothing more, nothing less.

What we stand for

  • Simplicity first — every feature must prove it makes the tool better at its primary job. If it does not, it does not ship.
  • Offline by default — if an app does not need the internet, it should not require it. Your data stays on your device.
  • Privacy as architecture — we design for zero data collection from day one, not as an afterthought or a premium feature.
  • One price, fair terms — free or buy once, own forever. No subscriptions, no upsells, no “premium” that is actually the real product.
  • Built in the open — our code is public, our decisions are transparent, and anyone can verify what we claim.

The long view

We are not looking for a quick exit. Arch Apps is built to last — as a sustainable collection of tools that people can rely on for years. We grow by being useful, not by being extractive.

If that means we stay small, that is fine. Small is sustainable. Small is honest. Small means every user matters.

We do not ask for your email. We do not sell your data. We do not hide features behind a paywall.
