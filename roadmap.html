01 Now — Wave 1 (Q3 2026) 4-6 weeks
Utility iOS / Android / Web

QR & Barcode Scanner ✓

Point, scan, done. No ads, no tracking, no permissions beyond the camera. Your scans stay on your device — a unique claim in a category where most scanners send data to ad networks.

  • QR code decoding (URL, text, WiFi, vCard)
  • UPC/EAN/Code128 barcode support
  • On-device scan history (100-entry LRU)
  • Zero network permission — full offline

Utility iOS / Android

Calculator (Scientific)

Does math. That's it. No ads, no data collection, no internet permission. A calculator has zero legitimate need for internet access — ours proves it.

  • Basic + scientific functions (sin, cos, log, sqrt, power, factorial)
  • Expression chaining with standard precedence
  • Calculation history (last 50 entries)
  • Memory functions (M+, M−, MR, MC)
