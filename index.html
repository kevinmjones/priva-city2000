02 Why Arch Apps

Every app we build answers one question: what is the simplest thing that works?

We do not ask for your email. We do not sell your data. We do not hide features behind a paywall. Each app is a single-purpose tool — the digital equivalent of a well-made hammer or a sharp knife.

If that sounds refreshingly boring, good. That is the point.
